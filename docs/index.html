Welcome to Western Grange

expand_more

Nestled amidst the serene countryside, Western Grange beckons with its tranquil charm and vibrant equestrian culture. Here, amidst sprawling landscapes and pristine facilities, riders of all ages and skill levels find a sanctuary to pursue their passion for horses.

From thrilling showjumping events to leisurely pony rides, invigorating hacking trails, and comprehensive livery services, Western Grange offers a diverse array of experiences tailored to meet the needs of every equine enthusiast. Join us at Western Grange, where the beauty of nature converges with the thrill of horsemanship, creating unforgettable moments and lasting connections.

Showjumping

Our arenas serve as the canvas for riders to showcase their prowess and camaraderie in a competitive yet supportive ambiance. Catering to novices and seasoned riders alike, our diverse classes promise an experience for all.

Pony Rides

Initiate young adventurers into the enchanting world of horseback riding with our amiable ponies. Designed for children of all ages, our pony rides offer a gentle introduction to equestrianism under the watchful guidance of our seasoned staff. Set against the backdrop of our scenic surroundings, these rides promise cherished memories and boundless joy.

Walks & Hacking

Embark on a journey of exploration as you traverse the verdant countryside surrounding Western Grange. Our guided hacking expeditions invite riders of varying proficiencies to immerse themselves in the splendor of nature. With a plethora of routes to choose from, each hack promises a unique adventure tailored to individual preferences and skill levels.

Livery

Entrust your equine companions to the care of our dedicated team with Western Grange's comprehensive livery services. Whether you seek full, part, or DIY livery, our bespoke care packages cater to the individual needs of each horse. From spacious stables to sprawling grazing paddocks, our facilities ensure optimal comfort and well-being for your horse. With onsite amenities including regular exercise regimes, grooming sessions, and access to veterinary care, your horse's welfare is our utmost priority at Western Grange.
